\subsection{Project Management Board}
The team used \textbf{GitHub} for branch-based collaboration, pull/merge workflow, and version traceability. Planning/tracking were managed on a shared board with status labels (\textit{To do}, \textit{In progress}, \textit{Completed}), assignees, priorities, and deadlines.

The board followed project milestones: formalization, solver implementation, then benchmarking and reporting. This reduced late rework and prioritized rubric-weighted items.
